Until I Found You | Lost & Found Management System

Your phone, wallet, or even your student ID—losing important items can be stressful and frustrating, especially when there’s no clear system in place to help students locate their belongings. Many rely on word-of-mouth, random social media posts, or scattered bulletin board notices, but these methods are unreliable and often ineffective. Without an organized lost-and-found process, students are left with little hope of retrieving their lost possessions. This common problem inspired the development of the "Until I Found You" Lost & Found Management System, a digital solution designed to simplify and improve the way lost items are reported and recovered on campus.

The system provides an easy-to-use platform where students can report missing items, browse found objects, and communicate with finders or owners—all in one convenient place. With features like categorized listings, image uploads, and a built-in messaging system, users can quickly identify and verify lost belongings. Real-time notifications alert students when an item matching their description is found, eliminating the need for endless searching. Additionally, a secure login system ensures that only verified users can interact on the platform, protecting personal information and preventing false claims. The responsive interface is designed to work smoothly on both computers and mobile devices, making it accessible anytime, anywhere.

More than just a lost-and-found tool, "Until I Found You" fosters a sense of community and responsibility among students. It encourages honesty by making it easy for finders to return lost items, reducing the number of unclaimed belongings left on campus. By streamlining the lost-and-found process, the system saves time, minimizes stress, and helps students recover their valuables more efficiently. Whether it’s a lost ID, a missing laptop, or a sentimental item, this system ensures that every student has a fair chance of finding what they’ve lost. With its user-friendly interface and innovative features, "Until I Found You" is more than just a system—it’s a solution that brings people together and makes campus life more secure and convenient.

How to Use the Lost & Found Management System

The Lost & Found Management System for UCC, Until I Found You, helps students and staff report, search for, and claim lost items efficiently. This guide explains how to navigate and use the system step by step.

1. Accessing the System

Start by visiting the homepage and clicking the “Get Started” button. You will be redirected to the main dashboard, where you can report a lost item or browse found items.

2. Reporting a Lost Item

If you’ve lost an item, click on “Report Lost Item” and provide the necessary details, including:

  • Item name and description
  • Location and date it was lost
  • Contact information
  • An image of the lost item (if available)

3. Searching for a Lost Item

Use the “Search Items” page to find your lost belongings. You can filter results by item type, location, or date found. Each listing will include a description and an image.

4. Claiming a Found Item

If your lost item is listed, click on it and select “Claim”. Provide proof of ownership and communicate with the finder through the built-in chat feature.

5. Submitting a Found Item

If you found an item, submit a report under the “Found Items” section. Include an image, date, and location details to help the owner recognize it.

6. Using the Chat Feature

The system offers a secure chat feature where finders and owners can coordinate safely without sharing personal contact details.
